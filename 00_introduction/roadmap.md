## General Tips

### Time Management
- **Beginner**: 1 chapter per week
- **Intermediate**: 2-3 chapters per week
- **Advanced**: Use as reference, deep dive on specific topics

### Effective Learning
1. Read theory first
2. Run and modify examples
3. Try exercises without looking at solutions
4. Review solutions and understand different approaches
5. Implement from scratch the next day

### When to Move On
- Comfortable explaining concepts to others
- Can solve exercises independently
- Understand edge cases and tradeoffs

### When to Review
- Struggling with dependent topics
- Can't solve exercises after 30-45 min
- Forgot key concepts from earlier chapters
